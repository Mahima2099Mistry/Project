E-Police Station

An E-Police Station website is a website that deals specifically with FIRs and Criminals. E-Police station is a generic term for police and government gencies, departments and other organizations in charge of policing Internet in a number of police. The major purposes of Internet Police, depending on the state, are fighting cybercrime, as well as censorship and propaganda.
